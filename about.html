Our Journey

Four decades of excellence in paint manufacturing

1982

Humble Beginnings

Amekicolor was founded by Jacques Rusirare in Kigali with a small production facility and a vision to create high-quality paints for the Rwandan market.

1995

First Major Expansion

We expanded our production capabilities and introduced water-based paints, becoming the first manufacturer in Rwanda to offer eco-friendly paint solutions.

2005

National Recognition

Amekicolor received the National Quality Excellence Award for our commitment to product quality and environmental responsibility.

2015

Modernization & Growth

We invested in state-of-the-art production technology and expanded our facility in Kicukiro, doubling our production capacity.

2023

Regional Expansion

Amekicolor began exporting to neighboring countries, sharing Rwandan quality craftsmanship across East Africa.

Our Leadership

Visionary leadership driving Rwandan innovation

Jacques Rusirare

Founder & Chief Executive Officer

With over 40 years of experience in the paint industry, Jacques Rusirare has been the driving force behind Amekicolor's success. His vision for creating a world-class paint manufacturing company in Rwanda has transformed the construction and home improvement landscape across the country.

Jacques holds a degree in Chemical Engineering from the University of Rwanda and completed advanced studies in polymer science in Belgium. His technical expertise combined with his business acumen has positioned Amekicolor as an industry leader.

"Our mission has always been to create paints that not only beautify spaces but also stand the test of time. We take pride in manufacturing products that Rwandans can trust for their homes and businesses."

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do
