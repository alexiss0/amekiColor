Our Story

Discover the journey of Rwanda's premier paint manufacturer

40+ Years
Experience

Over Four Decades of Excellence

Founded in 1982 by Jacques Rusirare, Amekicolor has grown from a small family business to become Rwanda's largest paint manufacturer. Located in Kicukiro, Kigali, we are proud to be at the heart of Rwanda's industrial development.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for homeowners, architects, and construction professionals across Rwanda.
