Technical Information

Professional guidance for perfect results

Surface Preparation

  • Clean surface thoroughly before application
  • Repair cracks and imperfections
  • Sand glossy surfaces for better adhesion
  • Apply primer to porous surfaces
  • Ensure surface is dry and dust-free

Application Guide

  • Stir paint thoroughly before use
  • Apply at temperatures between 10°C-35°C
  • Maintain 50-70% humidity for best results
  • Apply 2 coats for optimal coverage
  • Allow 4-6 hours drying between coats

Coverage Calculator

  • WeatherShield: 10m² per liter
  • WoodShield: 14m² per liter
  • PrimerPro: 15m² per liter
